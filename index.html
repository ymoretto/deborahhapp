What about me

I write for the screen and for the page. And sometimes, other things.

I am a UX and Screen Writer. I'm currently based in São Paulo, Brazil, but have lived so many places I can't really count.

I worked in two projects for Google, as a UX Writer for Google Primer and as a copywriter for marketing website Think with Google. I also wrote screenplays for Buddhist monk Monja Coen, brazilian TV celebrity Didi Wagner and my own YouTube series Dilemas de Gente Branca.

I majored in Media Studies at Unicamp and have a Masters Degree in Aesthetics and Art History at USP, where I studied photographer Francisco Aszmann's trajectory, supervised by Helouise Costa, PhD.
